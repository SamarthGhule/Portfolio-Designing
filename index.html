About Me

Student | NIT Jamshedpur (MCA'24)

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio.
I am Samarth, a passionate and driven Graphic Designer currently working as an Intern at Techsol DMS LLP.
I completed post graduation in Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at the prestigious National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur (NIT JSR).

As a Graphic Designer, I specialize in creating visually appealing designs that communicates effectively and captivates audience.
My expertise in tools like Adobe Creative Suite and Canva enables me to craft designs that are both aesthetic and functional, from impactful branding assets to user-centric interfaces.

With a keen interest in Designing, I’m constantly exploring the fusion of creativity and technology to craft seamless digital experiences.
In addition to my design endeavors, I have ventured into front-end development, learning technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
I also tried hands over Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) to enhance my analytical and logical abilities.
Additionally, I practiced with SQL queries, strengthening my database management knowledge.

Thank you! for visiting my portfolio.
I invite you to explore my work, projects, and designs as I continue to grow as a Design enthusiast.

Let’s collaborate to create remarkable digital experiences together!
